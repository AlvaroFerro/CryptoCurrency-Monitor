This app gives you the opportunity to monitor Bitcoin tendencies such as:

 

  • Bitcoin Circulation
  • Market Price in USD
  • Market Capitalization
  • Change USD Volumen
  • Total transactions per day
  • Total transactions

 

As we all know, some alternatives to Bitcoin have been appearing over the last few years. With this app you have the perfect alternative to compare between crypto currencies as well as with EUR, USD, JPY... and much more!

 

In general in this app you are able to control the following settings:

     

  1. Themes
  2. Type of chart to display
  3. Crypto currency to display
  4. Real currency to display and compare
  5. Time interval
  6. Forecast

 

Every single piece of data is extracted, cleaned and displayed in real time using API
